You have a limited number of attempts to find the secret combination. Each attempt must be composed of the available colors, and may contain the same color several times.
The number of attempts and colors available depends on the chosen diffilculty.

A location in an attempted combination can correspond to only one indicator (red, white or nothing). The order of the indicators obtained is random and therefore provides no additional information.

Example 1

Secret combination: yellow, green, red and blue
Combination attempted: green, magenta, white and blue
Indicators obtained: 1 red and 1 white

Explanation:

  • Blue gets a red indicator, because it's well-placed
  • Green gets a white indicator because it is present but poorly placed
  • Magenta and white get nothing, as they are not present in the secret combination

Example 2

Secret combination: yellow, green, blue and blue
Tried combination: green, green, white and blue
Indicators obtained: 2 reds

Explanation:

  • The second green and the blue get a red indicator each because they are well-placed.
  • The first green gets nothing, because the one in the secret combination gives a red.
  • White gets nothing, as it is not present in the secret combination.

Keyboard shortcut

  • Number pad: select a color for the active location
  • Left and right arrows: Change active location
  • Ctrl+N (⌘+N) : New game
  • Ctrl+E (⌘+E) : Help
  • Ctrl+Q (⌘+Q) : Quit
